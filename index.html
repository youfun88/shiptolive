What can you build for my business?

Four things: (1) mobile apps for iOS, Android, and Mac; (2) fast, SEO-ready websites and landing pages; (3) AI assistants and automations built into your existing workflow; and (4) SEO & GEO so both Google and AI engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity recommend your business.

What is GEO, and how is it different from SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps you rank in search results. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) makes sure AI assistants understand, trust, and recommend your business when someone asks them a question. I do both — structured data, clear answers, an llms.txt content layer, and the trust signals AI engines look for.

Yes.
